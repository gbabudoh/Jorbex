Legal & Compliance

Built for African & global regulations.

Full compliance with GDPR · NDPR (Nigeria) · POPIA (South Africa)

🔐

Consent at Signup

Terms + Privacy consent required. Optional marketing consent. Timestamp & IP stored server-side.

📤

Right to Portability

GDPR Art. 20 — one-click download of all personal data as a JSON file. Payment tokens stripped.

🗑️

Right to Erasure

GDPR Art. 17 — typed confirmation triggers permanent account deletion. Cascades all related data.

🍪 Cookie Policy

Full /cookie-policy page. Cookie consent banner with accept/decline. 4 cookie categories with legal basis per category.

🛡️ Security

Rate limiting on all auth endpoints (login, signup, password reset). bcrypt password hashing. JWT sessions. Webhook HMAC verification.

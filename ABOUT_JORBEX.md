# About Jorbex

**Jorbex** is a modern employment platform designed to empower African talent and employers by connecting qualified candidates with meaningful job opportunities across the continent.

## Mission

Empowering African talent and employers through innovative recruitment solutions that bridge the gap between job seekers and companies.

## For Candidates

- **Comprehensive Professional Profiles** - Create detailed profiles showcasing skills, work history, personal statements, and professional references
- **Aptitude Verification** - Take onboarding tests to verify capabilities and stand out as verified talent
- **Job Matching** - Get matched with relevant job opportunities based on skills and expertise
- **Career Resources** - Access employment resources, guides, and career development tools
- **Multi-Language Support** - Platform available in English, French, Arabic, and Portuguese to serve diverse African markets
- **Profile Strength Tracking** - Monitor profile completeness to maximize hiring chances

## For Employers

- **Job Posting & Management** - Post job listings and efficiently manage applications
- **Verified Talent Pool** - Access pre-screened candidates with verified aptitude scores
- **Candidate Evaluation** - Use aptitude scores and comprehensive profiles to evaluate candidates
- **Hiring Process Management** - Streamline the entire recruitment workflow
- **Flexible Pricing Plans** - Choose from Basic, Professional, or Enterprise plans based on hiring needs
- **Application Tracking** - Monitor and manage all job applications in one place

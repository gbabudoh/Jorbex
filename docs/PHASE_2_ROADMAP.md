# Jorbex: Phase 2 Strategic Roadmap (Q3 2026 - Q2 2027)
## From Recruitment Marketplace to Workforce Operating System

### Introduction
Phase 1 established Jorbex as the premier gateway for African talent discovery. **Phase 2** focuses on the "Day-to-Day" lifecycle of the employee, driving deep retention and high-value B2B revenue through **Jorbex Office (JO)**.

---

### Milestone 1: The Trust & Attendance Engine (Q3 2026)
**Goal**: Transition from "Hiring" to "Managing."
- **JO Core Launch**: Deployment of the `AttendanceSession` models and "Clock-in/out" functionality for PWA and Mobile.
- **Manual Overtime Tracking**: Ability for managers to review time-logs and approve excess hours.
- **Geofencing MVP**: IP-based location validation to prevent time-theft.

---

### Milestone 2: Managed Visibility & Real-Time Presence (Q4 2026)
**Goal**: Create a "Virtual Office" experience for remote teams.
- **LiveKit Status Integration**: Real-time Active/Idle/Offline indicators for the Employer Dashboard.
- **Smart Heartbeat Loop**: Low-latency status updates optimized for the African network landscape.
- **Employer Command Center**: A grid-view dashboard letting recruiters see their entire remote workforce in real-time.

---

### Milestone 3: The Payroll & EOR Loop (Q1 2027)
**Goal**: Automate the "Work-to-Wealth" cycle.
- **Frappe ERP Deep Sync**: Automated mapping of JO Time-Logs to Frappe Salary Slips.
- **One-Click Disbursement**: Integration with Paystack/Stripe to auto-pay based on JO attendance.
- **Compliance Guardrails**: Auto-flagging of labor law violations (e.g., maximum daily hours) based on the Employee's country.

---

### Milestone 4: Institutional Scaling & AI (Q2 2027)
**Goal**: National-scale deployment.
- **Programme Portal Expansion**: Letting Universities and Governments manage thousands of state-sponsored internships through the JO dashboard.
- **AI-Powered "Burnout Detection"**: Analyzing attendance patterns to predict employee churn or burnout before it happens.
- **Predictive Matching**: Fed by real-world performance data from JO (who works hard, who is always on time) to improve "Top Talent" recommendations.
